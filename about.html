We are Work In Progress (WIP) Magazine.

Why the name? We're reaching out to people like you and I - poly students who are soon blooming into young adults. The team at WIP believes we are works in progress: we continually get better over time. Wiser, more confident and more experienced.

We know adulthood can be a big, abstract and even scary concept. We want to be the bridge between you and your adulthood to make the transition as smooth-sailing as can be. The mountain of adulthood isn't all that scary if you have the tips and tricks to scale it.

At the same time, we want to help you make the most of the youth. Teenage years can be the best years of your life, so don't let them pass without you knowing.


Need advice on anything? We're always a line away! Drop us a question at our ask.fm page.


We love hearing your opinions, suggestions and feedback! And if you have enquiries, we're more than happy to help. Email us here.
